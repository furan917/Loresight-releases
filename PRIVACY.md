# Loresight Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-23_

Loresight is a fully offline tabletop-RPG companion. It is designed so that everything happens
**on your own device**. This policy explains what that means for your data.

## The short version

- **No accounts. No servers. No network.** Loresight has no backend. It does not create accounts,
  and it works with networking disabled.
- **We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.** There is no analytics, no
  telemetry, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.
- **Everything you create stays on your device** until you choose to delete or export it.

## Microphone

Loresight's optional GM Assistant and Notetaker features listen to the table to surface relevant
content and, if you enable it, produce a session transcript and notes.

- The microphone is used **only while you actively turn listening on**, and a clear indicator shows
  when it is active.
- Audio is **processed entirely on your device** by offline speech engines (Vosk and Whisper). It is
  **never uploaded** anywhere.
- **Raw audio is not stored.** It is processed in memory and discarded. Only the text you choose to
  keep (a transcript and/or consolidated notes) is saved, and it is saved **locally on your device**.

## Data stored on your device

The following are stored locally (in the app's local database / browser storage) and never leave your
device unless you explicitly export them:

- Your installed game-system content library.
- Session transcripts and notes you create with the Notetaker.
- Your settings and preferences.

You can delete this data at any time from within the app (Import/Export and Settings), or by
uninstalling the app.

## Children

Loresight does not collect any data, from anyone, including children.
